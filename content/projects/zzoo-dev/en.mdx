## The site is the case study

Most developer portfolios showcase projects. This one is the project.

zzoo.dev is built by the same AI workflow described in the blog — 29 skills, 7 agents, 3 departments operating through a [CLAUDE.md constitution](/en/blog/my-claude-code-setup). Every page you're reading was produced, security-reviewed, and verified by that system. The site isn't just a portfolio. It's a living proof that one person can ship a production-grade, multilingual product using AI-driven development.

## What makes this different

**Content architecture, not a template.** Each project in this portfolio has three document layers: a product brief, a case study, and a design doc. All frontmatter is validated through Zod schemas. Image paths are sanitized. Slugs are type-checked. This isn't a blog template with a projects page bolted on — it's a content system engineered for depth.

**Six languages, not two.** Every piece of content exists in English, Spanish, Portuguese, Indonesian, Japanese, and Korean — with automatic locale fallback when a translation is missing. That's not a feature flag. It's a content pipeline that scales.

**Built for three discovery channels.** SEO gets you indexed. AEO (JSON-LD schemas for `Person`, `WebSite`, `BlogPosting`, `SoftwareApplication`) gets you cited by answer engines. GEO (`llms.txt` + clean MDX) gets you represented accurately by generative AI. Most portfolios handle one of these. This handles all three.

## Open source

The entire codebase is open source. Clone it, study the content architecture, adapt the AI workflow. The real value isn't the template — it's the system behind it.
