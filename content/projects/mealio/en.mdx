MyFitnessPal: 30 seconds per entry. Lose It!: 15 seconds. Mealio: under 1 second.

The difference isn't a faster database. There is no database search. You open the app, the camera is ready where your thumb lands, you snap your meal, and the entry exists. That's it. Everything else — nutrition, ingredients, notes, ratings — is optional. Add detail when you want to, skip it when you don't.

A single photo is a complete meal record. That's the bet: **if logging is effortless, people actually keep doing it.**

## AI runs after you, not before you

Snap a photo and move on. In the background, Mealio's AI identifies ingredients, estimates calories and macros, and attaches a confidence score. Korean food gets Korean comments. The key: you never wait for AI, and you never need it to save an entry. If AI is down, your meal log isn't.

## No signup, no commitment

Guest mode stores 10 entries locally on your device. No email, no password, no OAuth prompts. Open the app, start logging. When you're ready for cloud sync and unlimited entries, signup takes 30 seconds and every guest entry migrates automatically. Nothing lost.
