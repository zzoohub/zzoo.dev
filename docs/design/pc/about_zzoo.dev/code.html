About

Hello, I'm Jin-Soo. I'm a full-stack developer based in the vibrant tech hub of Seoul. My journey in software has been driven by a relentless curiosity for how systems work at scale and a passion for crafting intuitive user interfaces.

After spending over seven years in the corporate engineering world, transitioning from hands-on coding to engineering leadership, I decided to take a different path. In early 2024, I embraced the solopreneur lifestyle. This shift has allowed me to reconnect with the code and focus deeply on what I love most: building products from zero to one.

Today, I specialize in designing robust web architectures for early-stage startups. I help founders translate their visions into scalable, maintainable, and high-performance applications. When I'm not buried in VS Code, you can find me exploring Seoul's coffee scene or contributing to open-source developer tools.
